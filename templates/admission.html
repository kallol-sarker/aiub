Admission Requirement

Undergraduate Program

 

1. For EEE/ IPE/ Architecture/ B.Science (CSE):

  1. A total GPA of 6.00 with a minimum GPA of 2.75 in Science background.  Math must be included at HSC level. (Optional for CIS).
  2. Minimum GPA 2.5 (in both O and A Levels separately) with no grade lower than ‘D’ among best 5 subjects in O' level and best 2 subjects in A' level. Math must be included at A' level (optional for CIS). (Scale: A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2 & E=1)
  3. High School Diploma/International Baccalaureate with minimum grade B in Math.
  4. GED result will not be accepted.

 

2. For Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA):

  1. A total GPA of 6.00 with a minimum GPA of 2.75.
  2. Minimum GPA 2.5 (in both O and A Levels separately) with no grade lower than ‘D’ among best 5 subjects in O' level and best 2 subjects in A' level. (Scale: A = 5, B = 4, C = 3, D = 2 & E= 1)
  3. High School Diploma/International Baccalaureate with minimum grade B.
  4. GED result will not be accepted.

3. For Arts & Social Science (BA in English or MMC/ BSS in Economics/ LLB ):

  1. A total GPA of 5.50 with a minimum GPA of 2.75 for Arts and Social Science;
    A total GPA of 5.50 with a minimum GPA of 2. 50 for LL.B.
  2. Minimum GPA 2.5 (in both O and A Levels separately) with no grade lower than ‘D’ among best 5 subjects in O' level and best 2 subjects in A' level. (Scale: A = 5, B = 4, C = 3, D = 2 & E= 1)
  3. High School Diploma/International Baccalaureate with minimum grade C.
  4. GED result will not be accepted.

Required Documents for Form Submission:

  1. Attested Photocopy of all Board Mark Sheets and Testimonials / Certificates.
  2. Three copies of Recent Colored & Clear Passport Sized Photographs.
  3. For O’Level &A’Level Students, Attested Photocopy of Certificates/Hard copies (with cash in code).
  4. A’ level candidates who will appear on November 2019 are required to submit photocopy of Statement of Entry and original hard copy/Mark sheets of their A’s level Exam(with cash in code).
  5. Applicants in Freedom Fighter category  are requested  to submit attested photocopy  of  (i) “Provisional Certificate” from Ministry of Liberation war affairs. (ii) Mukti Barta/Indian List/PM signed Certificate      (iii) Gazette  (iv) “National ID” of the freedom fighter & Birth Certificate / Death Certificate of the Freedom Fighter. (vi) Attested Internet Information of FF.

***Selected applicants in FF quota are requested to bring all these original papers during their admission.

 

Written Exam Waiver Conditions (will be called for viva interview):

  • Minimum score of 1650 in SAT for undergraduate program out of 2400
  • Minimum score of 1106 in SAT for undergraduate program out of 1600

Graduate Program

1. Requirements For Regular MBA /MDS/ MsCS / M.TEL/ MEEE:

  1. Minimum 3 Years of Bachelor Degree completion is required.
  2. Minimum 2nd division in SSC, HSC & Graduation from any of the disciplines or minimum GPA 2.5 in HSC & SSC (in the scale of 5) and minimum CGPA 2.5 in Bachelor Degree (in the scale of 4).
  3. For A Level & O Level Background, Minimum GPA 2.5 (in both O & A Levels separately) with no grade lower than‘D’among best 5 subjects in O'level and best 2 subjects in A' level.(Scale:A=5,B=4,C=3,D=2 &E=1)

 

  • Additional Requirements for MsCS:

4 Years Graduation degrees is required in CS, CSE, CIS,SE, CSSE, EEE, COE, ECE, MIS, Electronics, Mathematics, Physics, or in any other science or Technical disciplines (To be decided by Prog. Director)

 

  • Additional Requirements for MTEL/ MEEE:

4 Years Graduation degrees is required in EEE, COE, ECE, ETE, CS, CSE, CIS, SE, CSSE, App. Physics & Electronics, ICT, ICE, APEE, APECE or in any other science or Technical disciplines (decided by Prog.Director)

2. Requirements For EMBA:

  1. Graduation in any dicipline with minimum 2nd Division.
  2. At least 3 years working experience with current permanent job (case to case basis) in an office.

3. Requirements For MDS:

  1.  Graduation in any  discipline with Minimum CGPA 2.75 or 2nd class is required in Bachelor Degree.

4. Requirements For MPH:

  1. Graduation in any discipline of Health Science.
  2. Graduation in any discipline with work experience in Health Care Organization.
  3. Posses an M.B.B.S., B.D.S., M.D. or a 3-4 years Bachelor degree or any other equivalent degree.
  4. No 3rd division (Exception may be made for candidates with substantial amount of experience).

Required Documents for Form Submission:

  1. Attested Photocopy of all Board Mark Sheets and Testimonials / Certificates.
  2. Graduation’s Transcripts & Provisional Certificate Should Be Officially Issued. Student Copy is not Allowed
  3. Three Copies of Recent Colored & Clear Passport Sized Photographs.
  4. Job Certificates for EMBA applicant.
  5. For Appeared student (Only for National University) attested copy of Appeared Certificate & Previous 3 Years’ Results
  6. Applicants in Freedom Fighter category are requested  to submit attested photocopy  of  (i) “Provisional Certificate” from Ministry of Liberation war affairs. (ii) Mukti Barta/Indian List/PM signed Certificate      (iii) Gazette  (iv) “National ID” of the freedom fighter & Birth Certificate / Death Certificate of the Freedom Fighter. (v) Attested Internet Information of FF.

***Selected applicants in FF quota are requested to bring all these original papers during their admission.

 

Written Exam Waiver Conditions (will be called for viva interview):

  • AIUB Graduates with a CGPA of 3.0 or above in their Bachelors’ Degree
  • Minimum score of 550 in GMAT/ GRE for graduate program
  • 3 years of work experience in Supervisory level for MBA, VIVA only
  • 3 years work experience for EMBA program, VIVA only
